Marie Skłodowska-Curie Project
========

This website summarizes the Marie Skłodowska-Curie Individual Fellowship of Janine George "A materials informatics approach to the Pauling rules" with the acronym "JG-GH-UCLouvain" and the project number "837910" . The research was done at the [UCLouvain](https://www.uclouvain.be/) in Belgium from October 2019 to April 2021 under the supervision of [Geoffroy Hautier](https://perso.uclouvain.be/geoffroy.hautier/).
